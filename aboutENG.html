Synusia Studio is a mobile studio based in Angers (France), founded and operated by Hugo Fouassier, musician and audio tech (live/studio).

It offers recording services tailoring every recording spaces (studios, stages, atypical environments…) and workflows (live takes, partial live takes, overdubs/re-re…), but also mixing and mastering services. For every step of the process, a more or less significant participation in the artistic direction is possible.

In order to best prepare your recordings, it is also possible to plan pre-productions sessions, in close if not identical conditions to the recording sessions.

Projects falling outside the usual studio framework, such as the recording and mixing of liveshows, podcasts or even audio books are always welcome.

synusia, feminine noun, from the Ancient Greek "συνουσία" ("shared existence, gathered people"): founding principle of Plato's Academy, claiming the need of critical dialogue; also refers to the phytosociological designation of a temporary combination of living beings.
