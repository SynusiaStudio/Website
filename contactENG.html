To discuss your project and get a quote, I invite you to describe it to me in the most detailed way, in order to best identify and meet your needs and wishes.

The essential infos are, for example, the artist’s or band’s name, the type of service (pre-production, recording, mixing, mastering…), the number of tracks and overall project’s length, relevant dates/deadlines, the overall production budget (from pre-production to mastering) and if the project benefits from the support of a society (such as a record company).

A few more lines describing the project (influences/references, instrumental and vocal lineup, gear, live or overdubs…) would be appreciated!

All requests can be sent via mail to contact(at)synusiastudio.com.

(Out of respect for your project and this crucial step of a record’s production, I don’t master the projects I already mixed. However, I can recommend engineers to put these finishing touches to your record!)

synusia, feminine noun, from the Ancient Greek "συνουσία" ("shared existence, gathered people"): founding principle of Plato's Academy, claiming the need of critical dialogue; also refers to the phytosociological designation of a temporary combination of living beings.
